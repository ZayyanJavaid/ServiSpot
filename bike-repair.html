Bike Maintenance Tips

  • Check tire pressure before every ride for optimal performance and safety.
  • Clean and lubricate your chain regularly to prevent wear and rust.
  • Inspect brakes and gears frequently for smooth operation.
  • Tighten bolts and check for loose parts to avoid breakdowns.
  • Schedule regular tune-ups for a longer-lasting, safer bike.
