Tips for Homeowners

  • Hire a licensed electrician for all major electrical installations or repairs.
  • Get wiring inspected by a licensed expert every 2–3 years.
  • Avoid DIY fixes—always call a certified, licensed professional for safety.
  • Upgrade old circuits through a licensed electrician to prevent overload issues.
  • Use licensed technicians to install surge protectors and safety devices properly.
