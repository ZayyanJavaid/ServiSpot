Carpentry Tips

  • Dust and polish wood surfaces regularly.
  • Check for loose joints and fix them early.
  • Avoid water exposure to prevent wood damage.
  • Use felt pads under furniture to protect floors.
  • Schedule professional maintenance for lasting results.
